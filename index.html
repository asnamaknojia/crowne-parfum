OUR STORY

Crowne Parfum was born from a passion for creating unforgettable sensory experiences. Founded in 2010 by master perfumer Élise Laurent, our brand combines traditional French perfumery techniques with innovative modern accords.

Each fragrance in our collection tells a story - of distant lands, cherished memories, and dreams yet to be realized. We source only the finest ingredients from around the world to ensure every bottle of Crowne Parfum is a masterpiece.

Our commitment to sustainability and ethical sourcing means you can indulge in luxury with a clear conscience. From our eco-friendly packaging to our cruelty-free testing policies, every detail reflects our values.
